## üìä EVIDENCE HIGHLIGHTS

### Grooming & Child Exploitation
- **500,000 online predators** active daily
- **89% of sexual advances** toward children in chat/messaging
- **29+ million CSAM reports** to NCMEC annually
- **UK:** Documented grooming gangs **specifically targeting Sikh girls aged 13+**
- **Victims:** Groomed for 18+ months, blackmailed into prostitution, videos recorded

### School Bullying
- **77.5% of Sikh students** bullied (vs. 20% general population = 3.8x higher rate)
- **67% of turbaned Sikh youth** bullied
- **Articles of faith targeted:** Turbans forcibly removed, kirpans broken, hair cut
- **Physical bullying higher** for males with visible religious identity
- **Only 51%** see effective remediation when reporting to schools

### Sexual Abuse Underreporting
- **40% less likely** to report to police (South Asian communities vs. white British)
- **Only 3%** of South Asian abuse survivors obtained restraining orders
- **Only 11%** received counseling support
- **Police describe reporting as "negligible"** despite evidence abuse equally prevalent

### Cultural Barriers
- Sex is taboo ‚Üí Children lack grooming awareness
- Shame/honor ‚Üí Abuse kept secret; victims blamed
- Fear of authorities ‚Üí Won't report
- Language barriers ‚Üí Can't seek help or explain abuse
- Lack of culturally-competent services ‚Üí Mainstream therapy fails
