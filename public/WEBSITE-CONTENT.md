**Visual**: Phone screenshot showing camera pointed at Gurmukhi text on projector screen

### How It Works

**Step 1: Point Your Camera**
At the Gurdwara projector, YouTube video, or any Gurmukhi text.

**Step 2: AI Reads the Text**
Google Cloud Vision OCR extracts Gurmukhi characters.
Fuzzy matching handles typos and projector glare.

**Step 3: See the Shabad**
Gurmukhi text (SantLipi font)
English translation
Ang number (page in Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji)
Writer (Guru or Bhagat)

All in under 3 seconds.

---

### Features

**Fast**: <3 second identification
**Accurate**: 90%+ success rate on clear images
**Offline-ready**: Full SGGS database bundled (coming soon)
**Feedback Loop**: "Was this correct?" improves accuracy
**Bookmarks**: Save shabads for later (local storage)
